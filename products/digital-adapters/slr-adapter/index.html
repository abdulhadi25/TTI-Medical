Digital SLR Adapter

Home Products Digital Camera Adapters Digital SLR Adapter

February 20, 2020 by Ross Howes

The Digital SLR adapter connects cameras to Slit Lamps and Surgical Microscopes that can mount a beamsplitter. Interchangeable lenses and mounts make it compatible with almost any camera including 4k Blackmagic video cameras.

  • Compatible with APS-C, MFT, and Full Frame cameras
  • Focus-control to fine tune your image
  • X/Y adjustment to center your view
  • Adjustable iris to increase depth of field
  • Can mount to any Zeiss-style beamsplitter exit port
  • Interchangeable lenses and mounts for the widest range of camera compatibility
  • Footswitch available for Canon cameras

Digital SLR Adapter

Don’t get locked into your OEM’s integrated product. Experience 4k video and photography on your slit lamp or microscope with the freedom to choose and upgrade the camera. If you already have a camera it will likely be compatible, and if you need a camera we are a Blackmagic distributor. Depending on your camera features such as recording, live HDMI view, and wireless file transfer are available.

In addition to the iris, focus, and centration settings on the Accu-Beam you have full control of the camera settings. For Canon cameras we also offer a footswitch for hands-free photography.
